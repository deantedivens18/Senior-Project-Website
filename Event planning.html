What is a Event planner?

An event planner picks out where the tournament is being held at , they make sure all the players are active and have somewhere to stay at for that event. Event planners have to call around to make sure that the hotel has a certain amount of rooms available and also can make a gaming room for the players to practice in. Event planners also make a schedule for the whole event that the players and staff have to follow. An event planner makes over 50k a year.

How to become an Event Planner

  • Bachelor's degree in business communication
  • 2 year experience with out the degree
  • Communication skills
