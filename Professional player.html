What is a Professional player?

Professional player is a player who is competing in tournaments to win money and other prizes.Professional players also practice day and night to become great at that certain game they are interested in. Professional players have different types of salary they could be making it depends on the company they are with or the partnerships they have. Professional player makes around 50-70k a year not including the streams and sponsorships etc.

How to become a Professional Player

  • Practice
  • Choose a game that you are most interested in
  • Compete in tournaments
  • Have a PC or console with enough storage on it
  • Create a team to help you gain more audience
  • Stream on twich and upload on youtube
