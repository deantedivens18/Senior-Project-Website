What is a Analyst?

Analysts are experts that take in information and use it by providing stats for that particular team. A team analyst works with the coachs to help strategize and analyse the player strengths and weaknesses. Analysts make around 70-100k depending on your ranking and what all you do in that field.

How to become an Analyst

  • Skilled with numbers
  • Complete different programs in video game design
  • Practice and take notes in different games that a actual esport analyst do
