What is Shoutcaster?

Shoutcasters are esports commentators who provide running commentary during live matches, and usually engage in discussion before and after games. They are also required to interview players and other experts in esports. Shoutcasters make around 70k a year but if you don't pursue it in esports you would be making 37,000 a year.

How to become a Shoutcaster

  • Learn about the field you have to talk about and practice it untill you get it right
  • Improve vocabulary
  • Reach out on discord to some shoutcasters to help and improve your skills
  • Have to know the difference between Play-by-Play and Color
