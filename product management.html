What is Product management?

Product management in esports will analyse the market, develop a vision or goal for a specific product that would be presented in a pitch deck or powerpoints. Product managers will also make sure that everything is on schedule to launch at a certain time for the company. A product manager makes between $69,845 to $179, 451 per year.

How to become a Product Manager

  • Have to have a university degree in business management
  • 2+ years of experience
  • Ability to make sure you have all the data turned in on time
  • Make sure you can pitch to other company partners depending on the product
